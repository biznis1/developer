<font face="Verdana" size="2"><b>Note –</b></font></p>
<p style="line-height: 125%; text-indent: -20px; margin-left: 67px; margin-right: 5px">
<font face="Verdana" size="2">1.
 All liabilities accruing, other than return related liabilities, will 
be recorded in this ledger. Complete description of the transaction to 
be
recorded accordingly.</font><p style="line-height: 125%; text-indent: -20px; margin-left: 67px; margin-right: 5px">
<font face="Verdana" size="2">2. All payments made out of cash or credit ledger against the liabilities would be recorded accordingly.</font><p style="line-height: 125%; text-indent: -20px; margin-left: 67px; margin-right: 5px">
<font face="Verdana" size="2">3.
 Reduction or enhancement in the amount payable due to decision of 
appeal, rectification, revision, review etc. will be reflected here.</font><p style="line-height: 125%; text-indent: -20px; margin-left: 67px; margin-right: 5px">
<font face="Verdana" size="2">4.
 Negative balance can occur for a single Demand ID also if appeal is 
allowed/partly allowed. Overall closing balance may still be positive.</font><p style="line-height: 125%; text-indent: -20px; margin-left: 67px; margin-right: 5px">
<font face="Verdana" size="2">5.
 Refund of pre-deposit can be claimed for a particular demand ID if 
appeal is allowed even though the overall balance may still be positive
subject to the adjustment of the refund against any liability by the proper officer.</font><p style="line-height: 125%; text-indent: -20px; margin-left: 67px; margin-right: 5px">
<font face="Verdana" size="2">6. The closing balance in this part shall not have any effect on filing of return.</font><p style="line-height: 125%; text-indent: -20px; margin-left: 67px; margin-right: 5px">
<font face="Verdana" size="2">7.
 Reduction in amount of penalty would be automatic, based on payment 
made after show cause notice or within the time specified in the Act
or the rules.</font><p style="line-height: 125%; text-indent: -20px; margin-left: 67px; margin-right: 5px">
<font face="Verdana" size="2">8.
 Payment made against the show cause notice or any other payment made 
voluntarily shall be shown in the register at the time of making
payment through credit or cash ledger. Debit and credit entry will be created simultaneously.
