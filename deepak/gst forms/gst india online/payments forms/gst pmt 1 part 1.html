<p style="line-height: 125%; margin-left: 27px; margin-right: 5px">
<font face="Verdana" size="2"><b>Note –</b></font></p>
<p style="line-height: 125%; text-indent: -20px; margin-left: 67px; margin-right: 5px">
<font face="Verdana" size="2">1. All liabilities accruing due to return and 
payments made against the same will be recorded in this ledger.</font></p>
<p style="line-height: 125%; text-indent: -20px; margin-left: 67px; margin-right: 5px">
<font face="Verdana" size="2">2. Under description head - liabilities due to 
opting for composition, cancellation of registration will also be covered in 
this part. Such liabilities<br>
shall be populated in the liability register of the tax period in which the date 
of application or order falls, as the case may be.</font></p>
<p style="line-height: 125%; text-indent: -20px; margin-left: 67px; margin-right: 5px">
<font face="Verdana" size="2">3. Return shall be treated as invalid if closing 
balance is positive. Balance shall be worked out by reducing credit (amount 
paid) from the debit<br>
(amount payable).</font></p>
<p style="line-height: 125%; text-indent: -20px; margin-left: 67px; margin-right: 5px">
<font face="Verdana" size="2">4. Cess means cess levied under Goods and Services 
Tax (Compensation to States) Act, 2017.</font></p>
