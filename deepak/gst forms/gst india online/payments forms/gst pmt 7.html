<p align="left" style="margin-left: 47px"><b><font face="Verdana" size="2">Note 
–</font></b><p align="left" style="text-indent: -20px; line-height: 125%; margin-left: 107px; margin-right: 5px">
<font face="Verdana" size="2">1. The application is meant for the taxpayer where 
the amount intended to be paid is debited from his account but CIN has not been 
conveyed by bank to Common Portal or CIN has been generated but not reported by 
concerned bank.</font><p align="left" style="text-indent: -20px; line-height: 125%; margin-left: 107px; margin-right: 5px">
<font face="Verdana" size="2">2. The application may be filed if CIN is not 
conveyed within 24 hours of debit.</font><p align="left" style="text-indent: -20px; line-height: 125%; margin-left: 107px; margin-right: 5px">
<font face="Verdana" size="2">3. Common Portal shall forward the complaint to 
the Bank concerned and intimate the aggrieved person.</font><p align="left" style="text-indent: -20px; line-height: 125%; margin-left: 107px; margin-right: 5px">
<font face="Verdana" size="2">4. ‘CGST’ stands for Central Goods and Services 
Tax; ‘SGST’ stands for State Goods and Services Tax; ‘UTGST’ stands for Union 
territory Goods and Services Tax; ‘IGST’ stands for Integrated Goods and 
Services Tax and ‘Cess’ stands for Goods and Services Tax(Compensation to 
States).</font>
