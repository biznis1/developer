About Us



Press Monitor has been an invaluable ally to PR and corporate communication specialists for over 23 years. A company that changed the way the press is monitored in India. Forever.

Started in 1994, Press Monitor has provided press cuttings to corporates and government bodies all over India. Over the years we have expanded, gaining new clients from a broad range of industries. We have added more publications, more services and introduced technology that gets information to you in more formats.

We take pride in delivering timely monitoring services to a variety of organisations - from Fortune 500 companies to the government, from NGOs to PR agencies. As market leaders we continue to innovate and ensure we meet your changing needs.

We have not given in to problems inherent in providing a time-critical service. We have fought and prevailed. And successfully delivered on our promise and mission.

Press Monitor has been delivered to clients before 8 AM without fail. No delays. No excuses. Plain service.
