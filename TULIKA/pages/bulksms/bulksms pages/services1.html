What is email to SMS?

Email to SMS is a way to send an email that gets converted into an SMS. You can customize your dashboard and then send SMS directly to your intended recipients with the click of a button.


The Need for Email to SMS

Email to SMS is an add on feature capable of saving a great deal of time. The customer does not need to login into his customer panel. Most individuals are already logged into their email accounts at all times. The service saves time of users by allowing them to send SMS to a plethora of groups directly from their email client.

How the service works?

Email to SMS uses an API in the backend to convert emails into SMS messages. The system reads emails sent from authorized email addresses. It then removes any footers or unnecessary text and puts it in the pipeline as per group allocation.

This feature helps the users by allowing him to send SMS from gmail, yahoo, hotmail, outlook, email clients, etc. One can send SMS from CRM systems, softwares, and third party softwares. If a program is capable of sending an email, this feature allows it to send an SMS as well!
